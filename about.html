About Unity



Unity is a platform used to inform the public about the current terror crisis that may arise and encourage humanitarian aid. This was inspired by how there are many tragedies in the world that people are unaware of. On top of people becoming more aware of what is going on around them and how they can help, there are also social media widgets to connect with a younger audience. With the help of the Girls Who Code staff, and the resources provided by JPMorgan Chase, we were able to successfully create Unity.

Created by: Jacqueline, Nuran, Sofia, Katherin
